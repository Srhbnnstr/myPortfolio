To-Doodling

I created a productivity app as my final independent project. To-Doodling was created with Rails, PostgreSQL, Javascript, HTML5, CSS, Sass and Bootstrap. I wanted to create an app to manage productivity in an organized manner, but also making it aesthetically pleasing and enjoyable to use. To-Doodling is responsive to different devices, has a feature that allows users to jot down quick doodles to add to their lists and other convenient features such as a custom calendar to track tasks on a user's profile.
